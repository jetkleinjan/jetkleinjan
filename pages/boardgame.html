TBS Board Game

Following a long period of extensive research on TBS in the Netherlands, fellow illustrator Finn van Cappelle and I created a board game allowing players to experience the road of entering a mental health institute when sentenced unsuitable for prison. The game was intentionally designed to make the procedure intolerable, igniting anger and frustration in its players. The game was entirely designed from scratch; the board was built out of wood, all cards were designed digitally and printed and the rule booklet allowed for a smooth playing experience.
