15 Minute Poster

This poster is designed to raise awareness about a young man in Belarus, who has been sentenced to 5 years in prison proceeding an unfair trial. As part of an in class assignment, we were instructed to find ways in which we can aid organizations through our image making. I decided to choose Amnesty International as an organization, with the hope to hypothesize my contributions to the organization through my illustrative practices. This digital poster was created within a time limit fof 15 minutes, showing how even within a short deadline I manage to explore creative ways to tell someones story.
