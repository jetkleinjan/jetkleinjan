Grabbelton

This project was titled “Who am I?”. We were provided a cardboard box (40cmx40cmx50cm) that had to contain an in-depth artistic research about our identity as an image maker. The Grabbelton is an item present at dutch fairs and is reminiscent of my childhood. This project created an immersive experience for both myself and the audience that brings one back to the childhood experience of digging through a box filled with small gifts. The gifts retrieved from the game represented trinkets that are close to me as an illustrator. Each gift was wrapped in packaging that depicted either my artwork or something important to me.
