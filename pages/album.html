I Know You Know Album Cover

I was commissioned to design an album cover for two talented artists, Yunnee and Chris Sigl. Their song is titled ‘I Know You Know’, and after extensive discussions, I applied their demands to create a custom and unique cover. The cover depicts the two artists in their home city, Rotterdam (The Netherlands), and the illustration and custom colour palette represent the colourful, summery vibes of the song itself. The clients were extremely pleased with the results, and spread my art handle in order for me to grow my network among artists in Rotterdam. This experience allowed me to learn about commissions, pricing my art and communication between client and artists. I enjoyed being able to use my creativity towards a shared goal and was inspired to grow my network in order to expand my commission experience.
