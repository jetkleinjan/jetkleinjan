Space Walk

The last man on earth has left his decaying world behind in the hope to survive on the moon. An exhibition piece hoping to ignite discursive thoughts about the ways in which we treat our planet, and questioning what our future will look like. This piece is connected to an overarching theme titled ‘Out Of This World’: revealed by the way in which the character is leaving earth behind in the hope to survive elsewhere. This etching was carved out of multiple reused milk cartons, and then printed on both newspaper and hand made brown paper. This method created the desired gloomy and ominous effect that the story conveys.
